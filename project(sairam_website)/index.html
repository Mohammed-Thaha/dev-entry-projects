Sai Ram Engineering College is an autonomous institution located in the suburbs of Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. It was established in 1995 by Leo Muthu, founder chairman of Leo Muthu Educational Trust. The college is accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with a score of 3.37 and is ranked 123rd with a rating of AAAA.
